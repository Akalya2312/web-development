DESTINATION SUGGESTIONS

Hill Stations in India

India is home to many beautiful hill stations perfect for relaxation and adventure.

Manali (Himachal Pradesh) - Snow-capped mountains and adventure sports.

Darjeeling (West Bengal) - Famous for tea gardens and stunning views.

Munnar (Kerala) - Green tea plantations and serene environment.

Ooty (Tamil Nadu) - Known for its cool climate and beautiful landscapes.

Heritage Destinations in India

Explore India's rich cultural heritage through these historical destinations.

Jaipur (Rajasthan) -The Pink City, known for its forts and palaces.

Hampi (Karnataka) - Ancient ruins and beautiful rock formations.

Khajuraho (Madhya Pradesh) -Famous for intricate temple carvings.

Mysore (Karnataka) -Home to the grand Mysore Palace.
