Trip planning tips

Pack Smart: Carry only what you need, including weather-appropriate clothing, essential documents, and travel-size toiletries. A light backpack makes traveling easier.

Plan Your Budget: Set a spending limit for accommodation, food, and activities. Book flights and hotels in advance to get the best deals.

Stay Safe: Keep digital and physical copies of important documents like passports and visas. Be aware of local customs and emergency contacts.

Embrace Local Culture: Try traditional foods, learn basic phrases in the local language, and respect cultural practices for a more immersive experience.
