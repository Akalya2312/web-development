Trip Planning Tips

  • Pack Smart: Carry only what you need, including weather-appropriate clothing, essential documents, and travel-size toiletries.
  • Plan Your Budget: Set a spending limit for accommodation, food, and activities. Book flights and hotels in advance.
  • Stay Safe: Keep digital and physical copies of important documents. Be aware of local customs and emergency contacts.
  • Embrace Local Culture: Try traditional foods, learn basic phrases in the local language, and respect cultural practices.
